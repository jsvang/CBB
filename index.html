How to Play

Assemble an all-time college basketball starting five and see if they can go undefeated through March Madness.

1

🎰 Spin the slot machine

Each round, press SPIN to roll a random team and era (e.g. "Duke, 1990s"). Legend eras span full decades for classic programs.

2

📋 Draft your starting five

Pick one player from each spin. Fill all five positions: PG, SG, SF, PF, and C. Multi-position players can slot into any eligible spot.

3

🔄 Use re-rolls wisely

Don't like a spin? You get two re-rolls per run: one Re-roll Team (new team, same era) and one Re-roll Era (same team, new era). A team + era combo never repeats in a run.

4

🏆 Simulate your season

Once all five spots are filled, your team plays a full NCAA season — 30 regular-season games, the conference tournament, and up to six March Madness games. Go undefeated to win the national title.

📊 How scoring works

Your season is rated on PPG, RPG, APG, SPG, and BPG — era-adjusted so a 30 PPG scorer in the 1960s counts differently than today.

Premium positions count extra in Team Power — PG and C matter most. Landing legends and stacking stats across all five categories is the path to an undefeated run.
